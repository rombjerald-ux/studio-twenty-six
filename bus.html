What the bus can do

The bus is designed to unfold into a flexible making environment with outdoor work surfaces, curated stations, and a setup that can adapt to the event around it.

  • Painting and mixed media stations
  • Costume customization and wearable art setup
  • Sewing, embellishment, and handwork tables
  • School and youth workshop programming
  • Private event and festival activation format
  • A mobile extension of the Studio Twenty Six brand
