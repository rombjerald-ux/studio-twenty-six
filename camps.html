Weekly Themes

The new structure is artist-led, project-rich, and much more specific about what kids are actually doing across the week.

Week 1

Color & Joy

Inspired by Henri Matisse. Bold color, shape, collage, pattern, and a wearable-art Friday with a mini gallery show.

Week 2

Nature & Texture

Inspired by Georgia O'Keeffe. Flowers, contour drawing, texture studies, earth pigments, and nature-built sculpture.

Week 3

Street Art & Self Expression

Inspired by Jean-Michel Basquiat. Symbols, graffiti energy, layered surfaces, words, and a giant collaborative street-art wall.

Week 4

Dreams & Surrealism

Inspired by Salvador Dali. Dream journaling, clay creatures, fantasy dioramas, transformed objects, and puppet-theatre storytelling.

Week 5

The Big Art Studio

Inspired by Picasso and open studio week. Abstract faces, sculpture towers, giant painting, choice-day making, and a final parent gallery night.
