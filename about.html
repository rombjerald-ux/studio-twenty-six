Oakland Roots

A studio with warmth, grit, and real creative generosity.

Studio Twenty Six is being built as a place where art feels necessary, accessible, social, and beautifully hands-on. The focus right now is a studio that feels generous, active, and deeply rooted in Oakland creativity.

What we believe

Art should not feel gated, overly precious, or reserved for people with formal training. It should feel available, supported, well-designed, and deeply human.

Studio Twenty Six is meant to be a place where adults, kids, beginners, and practiced makers can all walk in and make something with serious tools and strong guidance.

The experience should feel generous: materials included, strong visual atmosphere, real instruction, and space for process instead of pressure.

The Studio Model

The studio itself is the main story: recurring programs, real instruction, and a space that people want to come back to week after week.

Studio first

The physical studio builds the brand.

Classes, camps, salons, workshops, and recurring community experiences create the cultural center of gravity.

Events + Groups

The studio can also host meaningful gatherings.

Private parties, team sessions, and collaborative workshops all fit naturally into the studio model without diluting the core identity.

Core Values

The Stitch language gave this page a better voice: less brochure, more intentional creative brand.

Accessible

Art is for people who already make things and people who are just starting.

Sliding scale support, approachable teaching, and no need to arrive already polished.

Generous

The experience should feel abundant, not nickel-and-dimed.

Materials, guidance, and atmosphere are part of the value, not add-ons stacked on top.

Community

Consistency builds belonging.

Weekly classes, recurring nights, and shared making create relationships and momentum over time.

Private Events + Partnerships

The site should also make it clear that this is not only a class studio. It can host and create experiences for groups.

Groups

Birthdays, private parties, and intimate creative gatherings.

A beautiful, guided making experience for people who want something more meaningful than a generic event package.

Partners

Corporate teams, schools, and community collaborations.

Studio Twenty Six can operate as both a local studio and a creative event partner with a stronger visual identity.

Want to collaborate?

If you want to plan a private event, talk about partnership ideas, or bring Studio Twenty Six into a larger community program, reach out directly.
