Make. Circle. Repeat.

Art is not enrichment. It is oxygen.

Studio Twenty Six is a maximalist creative sanctuary for adults, teens, and creative community. We treat art as the fifth pillar of health: a place to make together, feel something, embrace glorious mess, and keep access real.

Fifth pillar of health

Humans are designed to create, connect, move rhythmically, tell stories, and make meaning together.

Studio Twenty Six exists to replace numbness with awe, mess, and meaning through communal making in circles.
