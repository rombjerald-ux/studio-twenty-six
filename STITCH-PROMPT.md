## BRAND

**Name:** The Art Way
**Tagline:** Art for everyone.
**Mission Statement:** Art isn't a privilege, it's a necessity.
**Location:** Temescal, Oakland, CA
**Founded by:** Tess Seufferlein
**Vibe:** Accessible, bold, generous, community-first, warm, not precious, not corporate. Brooklyn art studio meets Oakland energy. Everyone belongs here.

**Supply philosophy:** Class fees include ALL consumable supplies. You never reach for something and get told "that costs extra." The shop is for the ITEM you're customizing (jacket, tee, bag) — bring your own or buy from us.

## DESIGN DIRECTION

### Colors
- **Background:** Warm off-white / cream (#F5F0EB)
- **Text:** Bold black (#1A1A1A)
- **Primary Accent:** Hot pink (#FF1493)
- **Secondary Accent:** Golden yellow (#E8B931)
- **Tertiary:** Sage green (#7D8B6E)
- **Dark sections:** Black (#1A1A1A) with cream text
- **Light sections:** White (#FFFFFF) or cream (#F5F0EB)

### Typography
- **Headings:** Space Grotesk (bold, modern sans-serif with personality)
- **Body:** Inter (clean, readable sans-serif)
- **No serif fonts.** Keep it contemporary and approachable.

### Photography Style (use placeholder images matching these descriptions)
- Warm, natural light
- Hands making things (close-ups of paint-stained hands, threading needles, mixing dye)
- Messy tables, colorful materials, works in progress
- People laughing, creating together, diverse ages and backgrounds
- Studio space: inviting, lived-in, supplies visible, not sterile
- Festival energy: outdoor, colorful, community

### Design Feel
- Mobile-first, responsive
- Clean with breathing room — not cluttered
- Bold headings, short paragraphs
- Cards for classes and art types
- Hot pink buttons and accents that pop against the cream
- Sticky navigation
- Active page highlighted in nav

## TECHNICAL NOTES

- Mobile-first responsive design
- Pages should load fast — no heavy frameworks needed
- Forms don't need to be functional yet (placeholder only)
- Use placeholder images that match the descriptions in brackets
- Keep the design clean with lots of whitespace
- Hot pink (#FF1493) should be used for: buttons, links, hover states, active nav, price text, accent headings
- Golden yellow (#E8B931) should be used for: secondary buttons, tagline in footer/hero, camp card borders, stat numbers
- Sage green (#7D8B6E) for Thursday Salon tags only
- Purple (#8B6F9E) for Sunday Restore tags only
