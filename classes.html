Weekly + Seasonal

Classes built for skill, play, and atmosphere.

Studio Twenty Six offers weekly classes, longer workshops, costume labs, painting nights, and rotating evening events for adults, teens, and creative beginners.

What You Can Make

The Stitch-style image language works especially well here because these classes should feel tactile, premium, and visually alive before someone even reads the schedule.

Painting

Acrylic, still life, surrealist painting, layered studies, and expressive mark making.

Costume Making

Burning Man, festivals, performance looks, accessories, and wearable art builds.

Tie Dye + Fabric Color

Bold textile workshops with strong visual payoff and easy event energy.

Drawing

Still life, contour, perspective, charcoal, and skill-building practice.

Sewing + Textiles

Learn the machine, alter garments, and build your own soft goods and details.

Jewelry + Beadwork

Bracelets, wire work, embroidery beadwork, and smaller-scale wearable pieces.
