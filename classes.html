Starting September 2026

A monthly studio rhythm that repeats, instead of a one-off calendar that people forget.

The schedule is not just September. September is the launch month. The real offer is a recurring monthly pattern: one open studio, two event nights, themed Tuesdays, one last-Sunday Messy Mornings session, and a fifth-Tuesday wildcard when the month allows.

How to read it

The structure repeats every month. The dates shift. The names stay the same. That means people learn the pattern once, then only need the fresh month posted as a flyer, story, or QR link.

In shorter months there is no fifth Tuesday, so `Ride or Dye` simply disappears from that month instead of making the calendar harder to understand.

Why The Calendar Works

The point of the monthly structure is that a flyer or Instagram post can make immediate sense: recurring named sessions first, actual dates second, and direct signup paths throughout.

Visual clarity

The pattern is now clear even before someone looks at a specific month.

People can understand the recurring rhythm first, then check the current month for exact dates.

Click-through

Every named night can now have its own detail page and signup path.

That keeps the calendar simple while still giving each offer enough room to explain itself.
